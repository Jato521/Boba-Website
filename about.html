Our Story

Our Mission

Nature brings us many resources. We take them for granted and forget that they are finite. That is why we believe in sustainably sourcing our products. By drinking from our prestigious menu, you will get not only a great refresher, but also a sense of fulfillment.

Our Values

In a globalized world, where everything is becoming more and more complex, it is important to recognize and appreciate the simplicity of life. With a cup of coffee or tea, made from sustainable sources, you can realize the beauty of simplicity. We believe in providing socially responsible drinks that satisfy not only your taste buds, but your moral senses!
